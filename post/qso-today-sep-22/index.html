HT + DTMF = Fun: Simple Digital Communication for Handheld Transceivers

Presentors: Zhemin Zhang KD2TAI

Slides | Github

Is it possible to send text and images by just dialing on your HT keypads? Have you ever wondered how the dial tones work? In this talk, we discuss a simple protocol to transfer data using your HTs by encoding information, including text, images, or arbitrary files, into DTMF tones: this minimal yet functioning setup serves as an introduction to modern digital communication systems.
