Flea at RPI - Electronics, PC, Radios!
Jan 21, 2024

Save the Date: W2SZ Flea at RPI 2024

Mark your calendars! The W2SZ Amateur Radio Club at RPI is excited to announce our upcoming electronics flea market event!

  • When: Saturday, March 30th, 2024
  • Where: Rensselaer Union McNeil Room (2nd floor lobby), 1761 15th St, Troy, NY 12180
  • What: Join us for a day packed with:
    • A bustling flea market showcasing a wide array of electronics, PC, radio, and unique vintage finds.
    • A special door prize to be awarded on the day of the event!
    • Meet your enthusiasts peers.

Whether you’re a student, a seasoned ham, or just curious about amateur radio, there’s something for everyone at the W2SZ Hamfest.

Stay tuned for updates!
