2025 Flea at RPI - Electronics, PC, Radios

Mark your calendars!

  • Spring: Saturday, Feb. 1st
  • Fall: Sep. (TBD)

Our first flea market attracted 200 student and local visitors in March 2024! The W2SZ Amateur Radio Club at RPI is excited to announce our second electronics flea market event!

  • When: Saturday, February 1st, 2025

  • Where: Rensselaer Union McNeil Room (2nd floor lobby), 1761 15th St, Troy, NY 12180

  • What: Join us for a day packed with:

    • A wide array of electronics, PC, radio, and unique vintage finds
    • Loads of door prizes
    • Hundreds of electronics enthusiasts peers
    • NEW Project show!
      • Poster session for individual & club projects
        • Showcase achievements
        • Recruit club member - from all majors!
        • Receive feedback on projects
        • Get new project ideas

  • Admission: Free student buyer admission with valid student ID.

    • General admission: $6, or $5 with a flyer/social media sharing.
    • Sellers (4x3 rectangular table):
      • Early bird (by Jan. 24th): $10 first table, then $5 per additional.
      • At the door: $15 first table, then $10 per additional.
